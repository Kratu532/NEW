The  'Queen of Crime' and our favourite Agatha Christie has written most of the best works in crime and detective novels.

She had written over sixty novels. Here we are trying to provide them all but we expect you to be patient 'cause it's gonna take time.
Untill then enjoy the ones provided here. 

Just click on the pictures below to download the books.

Hi I'm Sady, your host of this journey through Christie's gems. We are starting with her biggest creation that was awarded the title of "World's Favourite Christie", it's none other than And Then There Were None.

Personally this novel has been a creative satisfaction for me. It's not an easy feat to create the death of ten people and not make the reader feel ridiculous about the situation. But she managed to meticulously weave a story that was both thrilling and imaginative.

And as Sherlock says,"Your story is the most intresting Madame!"

The runner up of the title "World's Favourite Christie" has been Murder on the Orient Express. It's another masterpiece that will keep you at the edge of your chair untill you can finish it. And I can bet (spoiler) that you won't regret the death of the victim!  

Next lined up is the one which has already shot to fame due to being adopted as a movie by one of the biggest production companies in the world. Yeah, you got it right! It's Death on the Nile. 

Sad Cypress is a personal favourite. Though to some it may feel like more of a character story than a detective one but to me the story holds a dear place. Especially the woman who is convicted for murder! 

If you are up for a story where everyone just keeps gussing till the end and the revelation is so shocking that you need to wash your face to believe it then the Murder of Roger Ackroyd is the thing for you.

Mysterious Affair at Styles is the first Christie book which when published announced the arrival of a full fledged crime and detective novelist who was up to steal all charm from the normal books.
