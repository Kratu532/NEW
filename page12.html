Will Someone

Will someone let me out of this fearful world,
someone who can make those images clear
which got blurred with time.
Someone whom I can find doubtful never.
Who can be my friend forever.

Someone who can explain me the life, like it is.
Who can protect me from world
'nd teach me.

Will someone do me this favour,
Can help me survive the brutal.

Can give me courage to face this world.
Can be by my side forever.

But I think, these thoughts are absurd.
For there's no one with such feelings,
no one with such emotions.

There is no one such in this world,
It's only a thought, absymal.

Still I feel and have hope,
someone is there......
