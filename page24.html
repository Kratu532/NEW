Blinding sunlight of two stars lashed on the parched land. A vast expanse of dust covered land extended to the horizon. Harsh wind whipped brutally over his clothe wrapped face. Damion put a hand over his eyes and scanned the desolate land. Then went inside his spacecraft.

A few minutes later he came out, holding a piece of black mirror in his hand. Strange symbols were illuminated on it's surface. He tilted it in several positions moving to and fro untill a little beep sounded from it. Then he again went inside.

He stuffed some necessaries in his bag and slung it on his back. Then he went to the control board of his craft and clicked a few buttons. "Hide mode on", an automated voice said. Then he came out of his craft and started walking in a particular direction. 

Huge rocks that once boasted of their might were chiseled down by the dust laden wind to strange shapes. Their smooth but rough surface was a specimen of what a persistant wind could do.

Damion trudged forward, fighting the wind and glancing at his device from time to time. After an hour, he reached the side of a cliff. He rubbed off dust from it's surface and some symbols that were carved on it, came into view. He pressed a symbol with his finger and the cliff wall got depressed a little to show an arched door. He pushed opened the door and went inside.

He found himself standing inside a huge hall that was carved inside the cliff. A few of the pillars that supported it's ceiling were crumbled into pieces. And it's stone floor was cracked in places. The hall was completely empty except for a beautifully carved stone pedestal that stood in the middle. 

Damion went towards that pedestal and touched at few places with his finger. The symbols over it's surface shifted and arranged themselves in a straight line. "Earth", he murmured, his blue eyes shined.

He took out a glass piece from his bag and put it over those symbols. Then he lifted it up, the image of those symbols had fixed themselves over it.

He came back to his spacecraft and flew away from Thard.
