I Repent

The beauties of life,
Grows and Dies.

The bright sunflower,
blooms in solitude.

The black cloud,
sheds out it's tears.

The birds leave their babies,
in the lap of nature.

Rivers,
Oceans,
flow serenely,

And the sun burns itself.

She made beautiful trees for shade
and air to breathe,
and made us sentinals of her world.

And now repents at her fate,
for having expected too much.

She cries for survival,
but no one
listens!
